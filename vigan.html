Historic City of Vigan Established in the 16th century, Vigan is the best-preserved example of a planned Spanish colonial town in Asia.
Its architecture reflects the coming together of cultural elements from elsewhere in the Philippines,
from China and from Europe, resulting in a culture and townscape that have no parallel anywhere in East and South-East Asia.
