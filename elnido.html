El Nido Palawan – El Nido is a pretty popular town in the Philippines.
Situated in the archipelagic province of Palawan, El Nido has a natural splendor that will blow you.
I can say I traveled around Southeast Asia rather extensively so if I had to pick the most outstanding experience to tell you about as far as heavenly beaches are concerned, I’d pick el Nido without a doubt.
